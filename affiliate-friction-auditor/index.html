Affiliate Friction Auditor

In preparation · Private audits available

A focused audit for affiliate websites that detects CTA friction in commercial hubs and listing pages.

Core problem

Many affiliate sites already monetize destination posts, but their hub/listing pages make users open the post before they reach a clear offer or CTA. This can add unnecessary friction between commercial intent and monetized links.

What it checks

  • Commercial hub/listing pages
  • Internal destination posts
  • Whether destination posts already contain monetized links
  • Missed CTA opportunities in hub cards
  • Probable affiliate gaps
  • Prioritized implementation tasks

Private audit includes

  • Review of selected hubs/listings
  • Hub → destination opportunity matrix
  • Monetization status per destination
  • Suggested CTAs
  • Prioritized backlog
  • Optional ROI scenario model
  • Notes for implementation and measurement

Who it is for

  • Affiliate publishers
  • Niche site owners
  • SEO operators
  • Review/comparison sites
  • Agencies managing affiliate content

What it does not promise

  • No guaranteed revenue claims
  • No automatic SEO rankings
  • No replacement for affiliate program compliance review
  • No public software download yet

Private audit available on request

Affiliate Friction Auditor is currently prepared as a private audit service for selected websites.
